Lexo - Word Slide Puzzle

Lexo — Can You Unscramble the Chaos?

Lexo is a fresh take on word puzzles. Slide scrambled letter tiles into empty spaces to form valid words across every row. It is easy to learn, challenging to master, and designed to test your vocabulary, speed, and strategy.

Move tiles into adjacent empty spaces, rearrange them as often as needed, and complete every row before the 10-minute timer runs out. Lexo features online word validation, time-based scoring, progressive hints, global leaderboards, three difficulty levels, and fresh words in every game.

Think fast. Slide smart. Solve Lexo.
apps.apple.com/us/app/lexo-word-slide-puzzle

Turbo Roll

Turbo Roll is a fast-paced endless runner that challenges players to navigate a speeding tire through a vibrant techno-inspired world filled with obstacles, rewards, and increasing speed. Players must jump, double jump, boost, slow down, and react quickly to avoid hazards such as rocks, falling obstacles, and deadly barriers while collecting stars and diamonds to maximize their score. As the game progresses, the pace intensifies, testing both reflexes and timing. With smooth controls, dynamic visual effects, exciting power-ups, and high-score tracking, Turbo Roll delivers an engaging mobile gaming experience that combines action, strategy, and replay-ability. Designed and developed by Dhruv & Varghese, the game showcases our passion for creating fun, polished, and immersive interactive experiences.

apps.apple.com/us/app/turbo-roll

Snake Block & Chain

Snake Vortex is a modern reimagining of the classic Snake game that combines fast-paced action with strategic gameplay. Players guide their snake through increasingly challenging levels, collecting colorful blocks to grow longer while avoiding bombs, walls, and the ticking clock. Each level requires reaching a target score to unlock a swirling vortex that serves as the exit, creating an exciting race against time. Featuring smooth controls, vibrant visuals, dynamic animations, and progressively faster gameplay across five unique levels, Snake Vortex showcases my passion for creating engaging mobile games that are both fun to play and rewarding to master.

apps.apple.com/us/app/snake-block-chain

Doo-Doo Dash

Doo-Doo Dash is a lighthearted endless runner that combines fast-paced action with a playful sense of humor. Players race through busy city streets, jumping over obstacles, avoiding hazards, and earning points with every successful dodge. As the game progresses, the challenge intensifies—each collision slows your character down, and too many hits bring the run to an end. Featuring simple one-touch controls, exciting gameplay, and endless replayability, Doo-Doo Dash is perfect for quick gaming sessions while testing players' reflexes, timing, and endurance. Designed and developed by Dhruv & Varghese, the game reflects our passion for creating fun, engaging, and entertaining mobile experiences.
